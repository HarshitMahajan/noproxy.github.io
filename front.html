No Proxy

A QR code-based attendance tracking system offers fast and accurate attendance tracking. After logging in, a unique QR code is generated for each attendee, which can be quickly scanned by a smartphone or other device using a QR code reader app, automatically logging the attendee's attendance. This system streamlines operations, reduces administrative tasks, and provides real-time attendance data

                     Identification

The digital ID card contains personal information and offers a convenient and efficient way for users to verify their attendance. The system eliminates the need for proxy servers and offers enhanced security. It can also be used for access control, time and attendance management, and visitor management. This modern and efficient solution is a secure and reliable way to manage attendance tracking and related processes.

                         Generating QR

Our platform generates a QR code upon login, which serves as a virtual ID card and stores personal information for attendance tracking. This eliminates physical sign-in sheets and offers a modern and secure solution for personal information and attendance management. With this innovative approach, our platform ensures the safety and privacy of user data, making it a reliable option for organizations.

                     Marking attendance

With our QR code system, attendance tracking has never been easier. Simply scan your unique QR code on the respective scanner provided, and your attendance will be automatically marked. This eliminates the need for manual attendance tracking and minimizes errors. With this streamlined process, organizations can efficiently manage attendance tracking and focus on other important tasks.
